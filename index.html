ARK: Survival Evolved

ARK: Survival Evolved is a survival game where you try to survive a new world full of dinosaurs! There are many environments inside this new world. From calm, safe beaches, to dangerous redwoods, expansive oceans, and large, mysterious volcanoes.

In this game, you explore the world, going through its interesting story, and fighting bosses to complete the game. But, you will need companions to fight these bosses. You will need DINOSAURS!

There are over 479 dinosaurs waiting to be yours... or your worse nightmares. Make yourself an army! Craft better tools to harvest resources efficiently, armor to survive harsh weather conditions. Build your dream base. All to get ready to defeat these bosses.
